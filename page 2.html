Grotta Palazzese
Grotta Palazzese is a renowned restaurant nestled within a captivating
limestone cave overlooking the Adriatic Sea in Polignano a Mare,
Puglia, Italy.
This unique dining destination offers a truly unforgettable experience
where guests can savor exquisite Mediterranean cuisine while being
surrounded by natural beauty.
The restaurant's menu showcases the finest regional ingredients, expertly
prepared to tantalize the taste buds of diners from around the world.
With its picturesque setting and exceptional culinary offering, Grotta Palazzese
provides a one-of-a-kind dining experience that seamlessly blends
history, nature, and gastronomy.
